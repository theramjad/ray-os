Current production changes I'm working on, tracked per-video.

### 1. Pacing & Breathing Room

**Status:** Starting from Video 13
**Source:** Viewer feedback (Charles Bell, Seabryd Inc — 2026-03-04)

The delivery pace is too fast and screens flash by before viewers can read them. This is a pattern across AI training videos — slowing down is a differentiator.

**Changes:**
- [ ] Speak ~50% slower than current pace
- [ ] Let visuals linger 2-3 seconds after referencing them
- [ ] Keep natural pauses — don't edit out every breath
- [ ] Add clear section titles/breaks between segments

**Why it matters:** Charles has a team of engineers he wants to train with the videos but hasn't purchased because the team "found it impossible to absorb the information without pausing 10 to 20 times per video." Pacing is blocking sales, not just views.
